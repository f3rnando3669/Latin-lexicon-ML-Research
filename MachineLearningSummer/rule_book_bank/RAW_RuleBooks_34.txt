Define <IR> to be a defective argument type called Inappropriate Reason usually providing a reason that doesn't logically support the proposition (e.g., "Don't you want the best for your baby?")
Define <RR> to be a defective argument type called Redundant Reason usually saying the same thing as the proposition but in other words (e.g., "This legislation is sinful because it is the wrong thing to do.")
Define <WCB> to be a defective argument type called Weak Causal Basis usually offering a reason that is not compelling enough to support the proposition (e.g., "I had a real bad headache, then saw my doctor.
