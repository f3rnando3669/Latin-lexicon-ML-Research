The statement "The United States is the wealthiest nation in the world. So every American is wealthy." should be classified as <IR>, which stands for "Invalid Reasoning." This is because the conclusion that every American is wealthy does not logically follow from the fact that the United States, as a nation, is the wealthiest. Wealth distribution within the country can vary widely among individuals.
