The statement "Religion may have been wrong about a few things, but science has been wrong about many more things!" can be classified as a type <WCB> - Weak Comparative Argument. This is because it attempts to compare the validity of religion and science by suggesting that one has been more frequently wrong than the other, without providing substantial evidence or context for the claims being made.
