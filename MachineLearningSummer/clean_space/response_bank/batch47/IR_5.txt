The statement "Your Honor, the defendant has been able to provide no strong evidence to prove his innocence; therefore, you must find him guilty." can be classified as type **<FE>**, which stands for a **Fallacy of Evidence**. Specifically, this could be identified as an example of the **burden of proof fallacy**. In a legal context, the burden of proof is typically on the prosecution to prove the defendant's guilt, not on the defendant to prove their innocence.
