<IDAA> stands for "Identify Defective Argument Type and Apply". 

For the statement "Every time I wash my car, it rains," several defective argument types could potentially apply, but the most fitting one is:

**Insufficient Causal Basis <WCB>:**

This is because the statement implies a causal relationship between washing the car and it raining, without sufficient evidence to support such a causal link. The coincidence does not prove causation; just because two events happen together does not mean one causes the other.
