The defective argument type that applies to the statement "Marcus wants to go to a small community college close to home, but most of the kids in his class are applying to larger colleges out of state. Marcus decides that he should also apply to those colleges." is <FU> (Presumptuous Consensus). 

This is because Marcus is making his decision based on the actions of most of his peers rather than considering his own preferences and reasoning. Presumptuous Consensus refers to the flawed reasoning that assumes the majority opinion or action is correct simply because it is the majority, without proper justification.
