The defective argument type that applies to "claiming that an idea or belief is true simply because it is what most people believe" is <FU>, defined as Presumptuous Consensus. 

This fallacy occurs when an argument presumes that the general consensus or popularity of an idea inherently makes it true, without providing sufficient evidence or logical reasoning to support the truth of the idea itself.
