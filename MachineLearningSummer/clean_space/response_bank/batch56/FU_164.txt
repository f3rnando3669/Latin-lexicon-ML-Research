Based on the definitions provided, "You should listen to Ellie Goulding because everyone is listening to her music." can be classified as a <FU>, Presumptuous Consensus. This type of defective argument occurs when a proposition is assumed to be true because many people believe it or act on it.

Here, the argument suggests that the reason for listening to Ellie Goulding is that everyone else is listening to her music, relying on the assumption that popular consensus equates to a valid reason. This is a presumptuous consensus fallacy.
