The statement "ALL teenagers are irresponsible" is an example of the defective argument type called **Generalization** (<G>).

**Generalization** (<G>) refers to making a broad statement based on a limited amount of evidence. In this case, asserting that all teenagers are irresponsible based on observations of a few teenagers, without considering the diverse behaviors and responsibilities of the entire age group, leads to an unfair and unsupported conclusion.
