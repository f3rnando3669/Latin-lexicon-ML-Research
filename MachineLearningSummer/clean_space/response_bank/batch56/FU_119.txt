It seems like you are asking to identify the defective argument type in the statement: "Makes people feel as though everyone is doing it, so they should too."

This statement is an example of **<FU> Presumptuous Consensus**. It falls under this category because it relies on the assumption that if many people are doing something, it must be the correct or acceptable thing to do, appealing to a bandwagon effect without providing substantive evidence for why the action is genuinely good or reasonable.
