<IDAA> "Ghosts exist... because I saw a ghost in my closet," is an example of what logical fallacy?

This statement can be identified as being of defective argument type <G>, Generalization. The speaker is generalizing the existence of ghosts based on a single personal experience. This type of argument is flawed because it draws a broad conclusion from an insufficient amount of evidence.
