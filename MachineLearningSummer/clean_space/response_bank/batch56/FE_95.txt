The sentence "Jollibee or McDonalds for lunch?" is an example of the **False Dilemma** fallacy, also known as a **False Dichotomy**. This fallacy occurs when only two options are presented as being the only possible choices, when, in fact, there may be other alternatives available.

In this case, the question implies that the only available choices for lunch are Jollibee and McDonald's, excluding other possible options.
