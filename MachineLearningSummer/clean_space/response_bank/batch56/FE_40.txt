<IDAA> refers to the defective argument type called Ignoring Other Alternatives. This is a type of argument that presents only two options, neglecting other possible alternatives that could be considered. In the statement "We can either stop using cars or destroy the earth," the argument suggests a false dichotomy by only presenting two extreme options and ignoring other potential solutions, such as adopting alternative fuels, improving public transportation, increasing carpooling, or investing in electric vehicles.
