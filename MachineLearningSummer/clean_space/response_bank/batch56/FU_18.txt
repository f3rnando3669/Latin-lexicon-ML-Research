The argument "Everyone uses Social Media to fundraise, therefore there's no problem with asking for money on social media sites as long as it's going to charity" can be identified as showcasing the defective argument type <G> (Generalization).

Here's the breakdown:

- Generalization: The argument makes a broad claim based on limited evidence. It assumes that because "everyone" (a vague and unsupported generalization) is using social media to fundraise, this practice is inherently without problems as long as the money is for charity.
