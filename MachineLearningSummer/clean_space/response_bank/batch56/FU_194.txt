The statement "This happens when you assume that something is acceptable just because it is very common" corresponds to the defective argument type <FU>, which stands for Presumptuous Consensus. 

This is an example of the faulty reasoning where the truth of a proposition is assumed based on its popularity or the number of people who accept it, rather than on sound evidence or logical reasoning.
