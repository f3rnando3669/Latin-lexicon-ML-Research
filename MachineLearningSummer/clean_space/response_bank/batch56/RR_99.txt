2. **Incomplete Enumeration (FE)**:
   - The claim "because it's better than any other country" does not provide a thorough comparison. It skips over the details and nuances that would be essential in establishing such a definitive ranking.

3. **Inconclusive Reason (IR)**:
   - The reasoning given ("because it's better than any other country") is inconclusive. It does not provide specific criteria or metrics to support why America is considered superior in comparison to other countries.
